Robotic Painting

RARA robotic painting offers manufacturers a wide range of products and technology. Its flexibility and high­ performance increases finishing quality, consistency and throughput, while dramatically lowering operating costs and decreasing wasted material (reduces environmental impact).

Our hightech robots can be used for primer, base coat and top/clear coat, using water­based, solvent and UV paint systems. In addition, they can efficiently coat intricate parts with recesses, curved surfaces and frame­like shapes.

RARA's complete line with robot provides versatility and superior performance in standard industrial and automotive coating. RARA’s expertise in robotic painting outstand in controlling the significant parameters like air & fluid pressure, flow rate, specific gravity, viscosity, appropriate temperature, and more...

Screen Printing

RARA offers top quality and customer satisfaction on our entire range of printing solutions. Our services range from Graphic Engineering and all aspects of pre-press handling, Printing process, Die Cutting, Laminating, Packaging and Shipping.

Using conventional and UV screen printing technology, RARA creates cost-effective products such as as point of purchase displays, signs, posters, nameplates, nameplates identification, name badges,Graphic overlays and bumper stickers etc. Sizes that range from very small up to large format, RARA screen printing offers a distinct advantage when vibrant colors are required.

 

 

Pad Printing

RARA has a reputation for excellent pad printing services. We offer a complete in-house pad printing package, starting from in-house cliche etching to high quality finished printing on various types of substrates.

Premium inks, surface treatment techniques, equipment and proprietary processes assures high quality printing on ABS, Acrylic, Nylon, Polymide, Polycarbonate, Polypropylene, Polystyrene, Stainless Steel, and other surfaces along with many other unique materials. Our state-of-the-art production facility offers pre-press ,PMS color matching and post press.

 

Laser Etching

RARA provides contract laser engraving services to the automobile component manufacturers . High speed YAG and CO2 laser systems enable RARA to provide precise quality marking and job flexibility. With both types of lasers RARA has the equipment for any laser markable material.

The laser marking process is ideal for a variety of materials, surfaces, and compositions. Marking depth can be easily controlled allowing for fine, delicate marking as well as more aggressive forms of industrial marking. Laser marking is well suited to irregular surfaces where contact printing fails.
